Cathy Yam

Bio

Cathy Yam is a creative writer living in Hoboken, NJ. She loves writing fiction and nonfiction, and she has participated in numerous writing workshops. In her professional life, Cathy works as a Content Strategy Manager. An alumna of Cornell University, she received her Bachelor of Science in Industrial and Labor Relations and Minor in Spanish. Cathy is also an alumna of General Assembly, where she took classes in UX Design and Front-end Web Development (HTML and CSS). In her free time, Cathy enjoys watching films and running outdoors.
